Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to NeoFit: Fitness, Gym and Home Workout. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use NeoFit:

  • Account Information — Name, email address, and profile photo when you sign up or sign in with Google.
  • Fitness Data — Workout sessions, exercises completed, sets, reps, duration, and calories burned.
  • Nutrition Data — Food logs, meal types, calorie and macronutrient entries.
  • Health Metrics — Body weight logs you manually enter.
  • Hydration Data — Water intake logs you record.
  • Workout Schedule — Your planned workout days and categories.
  • Notification Preferences — Your reminder settings (workout, water, nutrition).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and personalise your fitness tracking experience.
  • Store and sync your data across sessions via Firebase (Google Cloud).
  • Send workout, nutrition, and hydration reminders you configure.
  • Calculate fitness metrics such as calories burned, streaks, and progress.
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access to your data is restricted strictly to your account — no other user can read your data.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

NeoFit uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Optional login method. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Displays ads within the app. AdMob Privacy Policy

5. Permissions Used

  • Notifications — To send workout, water, and nutrition reminders you set up.
  • Internet — To sync your data with the cloud.
  • Vibration — For notification alerts.

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or location.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

NeoFit is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

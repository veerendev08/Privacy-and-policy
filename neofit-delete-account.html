Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your NeoFit account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.

Option 1 — Delete Directly in the App

1
Open the NeoFit app and go to the Profile tab.
2
Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account".
3
Confirm the deletion. Your account and all data will be permanently removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request via Email

1
Send an email to devhimanshi08@gmail.com with the subject line:
"Account Deletion Request"
2
Include the email address linked to your NeoFit account (the one you used to sign up or sign in with Google).
3
We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account will remove all your workout history, nutrition logs, weight logs, water logs, workout schedule, and profile data. This cannot be undone.

Click the button below to open your email app with the request pre-filled:

✉️ Request Account Deletion
